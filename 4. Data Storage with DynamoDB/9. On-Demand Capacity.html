Since December 2018, DynamoDB also offers a new option regarding the provisioning of read & write capacities: The On-demand mode.

This mode is great if you have no idea about how much read and write capacity you will need. And you can of course always switch back to fixed limits, too.

If you do know which limits make most sense for you, the traditional "in-advance" provisioning will be cheaper.

You can learn more about this new feature here: https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/amazon-dynamodb-on-demand-no-capacity-planning-and-pay-per-request-pricing/
